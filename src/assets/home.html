Who We Are

                                             RVEIYA DYNAMICS PVT LTD is a Skill Development and EdTech company, established on 21st November 2024, and approved by Startup India and MSME. We specialize in providing affordable, flexible, and industry-relevant educational solutions through live online classes, internships, mentorship programs, and project based learning for students in BTech, MTech, BBA, BCA, and general degree programs. Our offerings include both software courses such as C-Langu, Full Stack Development, Machine Learning, and hardware courses like Embedded Systems and VLSI, all tailored to bridge the gap between academic education and real-world industry demands. With a mission to make high-quality education accessible, we focus on practical learning and job readiness, equipping students with the skills they need to succeed in today's competitive job market.

We are offering the below services:

RVEIYA DYNAMICS PVT LTD is committed to empowering students with industry-relevant skills and practical experience through a comprehensive range of services and features:


  • Affordable Courses: Access high-quality, industry-aligned training at cost-effective prices, tailored for students from diverse academic backgrounds.
  • Flexible Payment Plans: : Make education more accessible with convenient, term-wise installment options.
  • Live Online Classes: Engage in interactive, real-time learning with sessions led by seasoned industry professionals.
  • Internship Opportunities: Apply classroom knowledge in real-world scenarios through hands-on internships.
  • Project-Based Learning: Build your portfolio and gain practical experience by working on live, industry-grade projects.
  • One-on-One Mentorship:Receive personalized guidance to support your career growth and skill development.
  • Industry-Recognized Certifications: Enhance your employability with certifications upon successful course completion.
  • Comprehensive Course Offerings: Explore a wide curriculum that spans both software and hardware domains, including:
    • Software: C Programming, Python, Full Stack Development, Machine Learning
    • Hardware: Embedded Systems, VLSI
